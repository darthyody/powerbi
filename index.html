Fundamentals of Power BI¶

Introduction to Course¶

The Fundamentals of Power BI is designed to take a student with no experience and change him into a data reporting machine. Each day includes a variey of lively discussions and labs to help all feel involved and useful in the world of data-driven business. The course has been hand-crafted to tailor the needs to teach a student with no prior data analysis experience to not only build reports, but to create them with a purpose and a story. See all lessons included in the Course Overview.

Topics covered in course:
  • Power BI Desktop Overview
  • Making Visuals Easy-to-Read
  • Using R in Power BI
  • Introduction to DAX
  • and more!
